OVERVIEW OF BYETS


BACKGROUND


  1. The Building Youth Employability through Skills (BYETS) project will empower Bangladeshi youth with improved technical, employability and entrepreneurial skills, and connect them to income generating opportunities: jobs and self-employment.
  2. BYETS project is funded by the honorable EMBASSY OF NETHERLANDS and the project is implemented by the SWISSCONATCT
  3. The BYETS project has the vision to transform the youth manpower aged between 18-35 years. The energetic manpower resource is needed to be utilized to make Sustainable Economic Growth.

ADMISSION PROCESS


To enroll in any courses of BYETS Project, an applicant must complete the online 'Registration Form'. To be more specific, the trainee selection and enrolment process will go through the following steps.

STEP 1: ONLINE REGISTRATION

Interested applicant can complete online registration using online registration platform of PencilBox Training Institute. For online registration, applicants need to provide following information:


  • Personal details
  • Educational qualification
  • All the related information of the trainee

STEP 2: SHORT LISTING

Interested applicants will be invited for face-to-face interviews. Selected candidates will be notified for enrollment.


STEP 3: PRE TRAINING ORIENTATION WORKSHOP

Selected candidates will be called for a pre-training orientation workshop where they will get the overall information about the BYETS project and all the necessary information.


STEP 4: FINAL ENROLMENT

Selected applicants will be duly enrolled on submission of necessary records including

  • Photocopy of NID/BRC
  • Documents relating to academic qualification
